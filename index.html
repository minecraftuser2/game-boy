Emulator: Game Boy, NES & DOS


Instructions:
- For Game Boy: upload a .gb ROM.
- For NES: upload a .nes ROM.
- For DOS: just select DOS and you'll get a command prompt.
   To mount an .iso, select DOS, upload your ISO, and follow the instructions.
No games are included on this page. Please use your own files.
Controls: Keyboard (Z/X/Enter/Shift/Arrows), controller (Xbox, etc.) for NES/GB. Mouse/keyboard for DOS.
